# Tax Filing Assistant

A simple, conversational tax filing tool for small and medium-scale business owners. Built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, styled with Atlassian Design System principles.

## Features

- **Mobile-only responsive design** - Optimized for mobile devices with a clean, modern interface
- **Conversational flow** - Step-by-step questions guide you through tax preparation
- **Business information collection**:
  - Business location and structure
  - Annual revenue and expenses
  - Team size
  - Bookkeeping tools
- **Summary generation** - Get a personalized checklist you can share with your accountant
- **Export functionality** - Copy your summary to clipboard

## Usage Flow

1. **Introduction** - Read the welcome message and click "Let's get started"
2. **Business Basics** - Select your country and business structure
3. **Financials** - Enter approximate annual revenue and expenses
4. **Team** - Specify how many people work in your business
5. **Tools** - Select your bookkeeping tool (if any)
6. **Summary** - Review your personalized checklist and export it

## Notes

- This tool helps you **prepare** for tax filing but does not submit returns
- Always consult a qualified tax professional or local tax authority before filing
- No data is stored permanently - everything is processed in your browser
- You can go back and adjust answers at any time
